ADHARVA 2025

July 12th, 2025 | Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan Manvila

Competitive Events

Natya Sutra

• 8min performance + 2min setup

• Max 8 participants

Rules:

• Standard lighting only

• Controversial costumes/topics prohibited

• Immediate disqualification for violations

• Soundtrack must be submitted at registration

• Props must not damage school property

Yukti (Quiz)

• 2 Teams/School (2 members each)


Rounds:

Round 1: 20 written questions (30 mins)

Round 2: Oral answers (20s/question)

Round 3: Buzzer round (-1 for wrong answers)

Round 4: Picture round (15s/question)

Naada Nirvana

• 8min performance + 2min setup

• 2 Vocalists + 3 Instrumentalists min

• Devotional songs prohibited

• School provides drums, IEMs, mics

• Max 9 members (4 vocal + 5 instrumental)

• Time deduction for setup overages

Nataka

• 3 Members/Team

• Props must be school-approved

Rounds:

Round 1: 30min plot development

Round 2: 1hr script writing

Round 3: 15min prep + 15min performance

Nazakat

• 6min Stage Time/Team

• 3 Models + 3 Designers

• 3 Costumes + 1 Prop minimum

• Black base clothing required

• Music must be submitted 1 day prior

• Theme announced post-inauguration
